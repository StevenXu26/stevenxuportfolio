Around Us

My graduation work is an audio quiz designed to teach people the proper gestures to use when interacting with blind individuals.

The quiz consists of audio questions and multiple-choice answers that users can answer using their smartphones. The project aims to raise awareness and reduce the social barriers faced by people with visual impairments.

  • Sound editing - Adobe Audition
  • Video animation - After Effect
  • Website - HTML/CSS
