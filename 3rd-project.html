Logo

As an aspiring designer, I have developed a passion for logo design. There's something about creating a visual identity for a brand that excites me. Through my internship, I had the opportunity to work on various logo design projects, and I found it to be both challenging and rewarding.

I enjoy the process of researching the brand and understanding its values and mission. From there, I can start brainstorming ideas and sketching out concepts. It's always a thrill to see an idea come to life and evolve into a polished final product.
