UX/UI Design: Electric car app


This mobile app for electric cars is designed to provide drivers with an all-in-one solution for monitoring their vehicle's battery and energy consumption. The app includes a dashboard that displays real-time information about the car's battery level, climate control settings, traffic conditions, and nearby charging stations.
